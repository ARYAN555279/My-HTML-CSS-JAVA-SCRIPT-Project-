Jujutsu Kaisen Season 1


Jujutsu Kaisen Season 1 follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities, who finds himself pulled into the dangerous world of curses after he accidentally consumes a powerful cursed object—a finger belonging to the powerful curse Ryomen Sukuna. Yuji joins the Tokyo Jujutsu High School under the guidance of jujutsu sorcerer Satoru Gojo to learn how to control his newfound cursed energy and survive the world of curses.





Yuji Itadori



< br />

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a high school student with an extraordinary physical strength and agility, despite not having any formal jujutsu training at the beginning of the series. Yuji’s life takes a dramatic turn when he encounters a cursed object—a finger belonging to the ancient, powerful curse Ryomen Sukuna. In a desperate attempt to save his friends from a cursed spirit, Yuji swallows the finger, becoming Sukuna's vessel.




Gojo Satoru



Gojo is renowned as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world, possessing immense cursed energy and a powerful technique called the Limitless, which allows him to manipulate space at a molecular level. His eyes, known as the Six Eyes, grant him extraordinary perception, allowing him to see cursed energy in great detail, further enhancing his techniques. Gojo is considered the strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer in the world, with powers rivaling those of the King of Curses, Sukuna.





Ryomen Sukuna



Ryomen Sukuna He’s known as the "King of Curses" and is an ancient, immensely powerful curse who was once a human sorcerer in the past. In the world of Jujutsu Kaisen, Sukuna is feared and revered due to his overwhelming strength and cruelty. When Sukuna was alive, he was so powerful that his body couldn't be fully destroyed after his death. Instead, it was preserved in twenty pieces—his fingers—which contain remnants of his cursed energy. These fingers became potent cursed objects that could not be eradicated.





Toji Fushiguro



Toji Fushiguro, also known as Toji Zenin, is a powerful character in Jujutsu Kaisen who serves as an antagonist in the "Hidden Inventory" arc. He was once part of the Zenin Clan, one of the prestigious sorcerer families in the jujutsu world, but he abandoned the clan and renounced his family name due to his harsh treatment as a child. He later took on the surname "Fushiguro" after marrying and fathering a son, Megumi Fushiguro, whom he later left behind.





Nobara Kugisaki



Nobara Kugisaki is one of the main characters in Jujutsu Kaisen, and a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High alongside Yuji Itadori and Megumi Fushiguro. Known for her fiery personality, Nobara is confident, bold, and unafraid to speak her mind. She has a strong sense of self and refuses to compromise her beliefs, embodying both strength and independence. Nobara’s fighting style is unique among jujutsu sorcerers. She uses a Straw Doll Technique, which involves a hammer, nails, and straw dolls to exorcise curses in a highly physical and creative way.





Megumi Fushiguro



Megumi is known for his unique jujutsu technique, the "Ten Shadows Technique," which allows him to summon powerful shikigami, or spirit animals, to fight alongside him. Among these is the Divine Dog, which he uses frequently in battles. His character is also marked by a strong sense of justice and a desire to protect others, stemming from a challenging family background.
